    Xiao Xu @ HIT-SCIR

    I am Xiao Xu, a Ph.D. student from Harbin Institute of Technology, where I am a member of Language Analysis Group of Research Center for Social Computing and Information Retrieval (HIT-SCIR).

    Fortunately, I am advised by Prof. Wanxiang Che.

    Love music, singing, animation, and all good things in my life.
